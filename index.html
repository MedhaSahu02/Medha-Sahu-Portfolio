The Brief

The challenge was to develop a modern, architectural brand identity for a premium design studio. The identity needed to reflect their core philosophy: stripping away the non-essential to let the work speak for itself.

The Strategy

We focused on an editorial approach, utilizing extreme whitespace, a monochromatic palette, and rigid structural alignment. The strategy positioned the brand not just as a service provider, but as a curator of high-end design experiences.
